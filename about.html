Introduction

Hello, this is the webmaster of SkyCloud, Index~

This paragraph has nothing to do with the services provided by SkyCloud, but Index still hopes that you can take the time to read it and read it as a story.

Ahem, okay, now I will give a brief introduction about SkyCloud!

SkyCloud server is a simple survival platform for Minecraft players around the world.

As you can see, everything on this server is designed to solve the complex and commercial problems of the current Minecraft server.

The servers are all high-speed servers optimized for speed, so that you can join the server faster if you read this text.

Although I nag that these are useless, in the end just for everyone to have fun.

If you are satisfied, I will be satisfied.

On the evening of July 10, 2020, I used the SpongeForge server to turn on the first SkyCloud server. At that time, I just hung up on QQ to promote it. Originally, I only planned to spread it among friends in a small area, but I didn’t expect it to spread widely and far exceeded expectations... So I made up my mind to continue this server. .

It has been almost a year since then. Inviting some old players, the access of the music game group, etc., have made SkyCloud spread more and more widely, reaching a level of spread I have never thought of.

No matter so much. Anyway, I still have to do it, and it will be even more.
